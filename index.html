About PyTennessee

PyTennessee is a regional Python conference held every year in Nashville, TN. We feature 3 talk tracks, a tutorial track, 2 keynotes, a day of Young Coders, and the always-delightful hallway track. Come join 300 of your closest Python friends and learn some more about Python and Python-adjacent technologies. We can't wait to see you in Nashville!
